# Accept Robinhood Read-Only Alternative

## Rationale

The read-only data source is the lowest-risk way to use Robinhood MCP and
respects the safety boundaries without account action.

## Before

Both Robinhood alternatives were proposed and unresolved.

## After

The read-only alternative is accepted. The live-broker alternative is deferred.
