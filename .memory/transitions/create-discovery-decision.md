# Create Discovery Decision

## Rationale

The funnel approach and the multi-factor screen are material design choices that
needed a recorded decision.

## Before

AIfund had no discovery stage or recorded approach for it.

## After

AIfund records the decision to implement discovery as a funnel whose first stage
is a deterministic multi-factor screen over a universe.
