# Codify Assumption And Constraint Convention

## Rationale

Backfilling fixed the existing debt; a convention prevents it from recurring.

## Before

No guidance said to capture a Decision's embedded beliefs and boundaries.

## After

`AGENTS.md` and `.memory/README.md` state that when a Decision embeds a belief or
a boundary, the belief is recorded as an Assumption and the boundary as a
Constraint, linked with `[[id]]`.

- `AGENTS.md`
- `.memory/README.md`
