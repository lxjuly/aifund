# Outputs Are Decision Support, Not Advice

Discovery scores, holding ratings, and research decisions are signals for a human
to weigh, not financial advice. Nothing trades off them automatically.

Discovery and rating never place orders. Only the guarded execution layer may, and
only in paper or simulated mode by default.
