# yfinance Data Is Adequate

yfinance prices and fundamentals are complete and accurate enough for screening
and rating.

Fundamentals such as P/E and return on equity are best-effort and sometimes
missing; missing factors are skipped rather than trusted. A more reliable data
source may be needed before acting on close calls.

Relied on by [[discovery-funnel-multifactor-screener]] and [[holdings-sell-rating]].
