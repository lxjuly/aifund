# Factor Signals Are Informative

Momentum, value, quality, and trend carry enough signal to rank buy candidates and
to flag sell candidates.

This is the basis of the screener and the rating. It is an assumption, not a
proven fact: the weights and thresholds are tunable and may need revisiting.

Relied on by [[discovery-funnel-multifactor-screener]] and [[holdings-sell-rating]].
